ABOUT US

DASH was formed in 2004 with a vision to innovate the earthmoving equipment hire industry and also changing the construction prototypes and becoming India’s one of the best construction agencies. After nearly 17 years of industry-leading service, DASH now services the wider regions of Banglore, Delhi, and Kolkata – providing ‘The Best Service on India.’

Our foundations were built around the customer experience with a key understanding of project scope, operational needs, and unique circumstances. We are dedicated to the constant approval of our services, aiming to provide you with leading earthmoving equipment, Architecture blueprint, control, and construction of projects of all kinds.
